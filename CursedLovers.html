Cursed Lovers


Cursed Lovers is a cooperative 3rd person shooter in multiplayer mode that was realized during the fall of 2022 on Unreal Engine 5.


In this game, there can be up to 5 teams of 2 players; in each team there is a Red player and a Blue player who will be bound together by powerful feelings and will not be able to move more than 3 meters from each other!


In order to spice up the fight, the reds will only be able to hit the other reds and the same for the blues. When a player is hit, he becomes cursed (off-pitch) and can only come back if he manages to hit a player of his color still present on the pitch. Victory is declared when both members of the same team are still on the field while the others are off ! (or at the end of the timer)
