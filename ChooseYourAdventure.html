ChooseYourAdventure


ChooseYourAdventure is an interactive RPG which was designed in the summer of 2022 on Unity3D.


The player embodies a garrison soldier in pursuit of a rebel troop. He will have to help the local population in order to obtain clues about the renegades while facing the various monsters that inhabit these lands.


The player interacts with the NPCs using keywords that allow him to progress in the story, in the various quests but also with the aim of improving his characteristics or learning new spells. Be careful ! Some choices will change the course of the story and/or modify the power of your character...
